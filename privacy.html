Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Custom URL/slug (optional)
  • Password (stored encrypted)
  • Age attestation (confirmation that you are 18+)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the CamBridge service
  • To authenticate your account
  • To generate your unique room URL
  • To comply with legal requirements

3. Video Calls

Important: Video calls on CamBridge are peer-to-peer. We do not record, store, or monitor your video conversations. Video data is transmitted directly between participants.

4. Data Storage

  • Account data is stored securely in our database
  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt hashing
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • With service providers necessary to operate the platform (e.g., video infrastructure)

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal session cookies for authentication. We do not use advertising or analytics tracking.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Update your account information

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information from our systems.

9. Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us through the platform.
